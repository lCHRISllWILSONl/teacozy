About Us

At Tea-cozzy, we curate artisan teas sourced sustainably from local farmers. Join us on a flavorful journey!

Our Story

Tea-cozzy was founded in 2020 by a group of tea enthusiasts who sought to share their love for artisan blends. Our journey began with a passion for quality and a commitment to sustainability. We believe every cup tells a unique story, and our carefully sourced teas come from local farms that support their communities. With each sip, you embark on a flavorful adventure, discovering the rich traditions and craftsmanship behind our teas. Join us in celebrating the art of tea!
